### Full Description (4000 chars max)
Street Shield is your AI-powered personal safety awareness companion — designed for pedestrians, runners, cyclists, students, commuters, and anyone who wants to stay informed and connected while on the move.

🛡️ REAL-TIME SAFETY AWARENESS
Get an AI-calculated safety awareness score based on your surroundings, time of day, location, lighting, and environmental factors. Street Shield continuously scans and keeps you informed — so you can focus on your journey.

⚡ QUICK ALERT TO TRUSTED CONTACTS
One tap sends your live location to your trusted contacts instantly. Set up a secret voice trigger word so you can send alerts hands-free — without even looking at your phone. Perfect for situations where you feel uneasy.

📍 LIVE LOCATION SHARING
Share your real-time position with family, friends, or housemates. They can follow your route as you walk home, go for a run, or cycle through the city.

🚲 CYCLING SAFETY MODE
Specialised awareness mode for cyclists. Get alerts about vehicle proximity, road conditions, and hazard detection. Track your speed, distance, and route safety in real time.

🛴 SILENT VEHICLE DETECTION
Electric scooters and e-bikes are nearly silent. Street Shield uses AI to warn you when fast-moving quiet vehicles are nearby — so you're never caught off guard.

🎙️ VOICE-ACTIVATED COMMANDS
Say "Street Shield" to get instant voice updates on your safety score, location, and environmental conditions — all hands-free.

📊 ACTIVITY INSIGHTS
Track your movement rhythm, energy levels, and alertness. Understand how your body responds during walks, runs, and rides.

🌍 MULTI-LANGUAGE SUPPORT
Available in English, Spanish, French, German, and Chinese. Street Shield speaks your language wherever you travel.

📴 OFFLINE SAFETY MODE
Core safety features work even without an internet connection. Your protection doesn't stop when your signal does.

📤 "I GOT HOME SAFE"
After your journey, share a beautiful summary card with your route, stats, and safety score. Let your loved ones know you arrived safely with #IGotHomeSafe.

WHO IS STREET SHIELD FOR?
• Pedestrians walking home alone at night
• Runners and joggers who want to stay aware
• Cyclists commuting through busy streets
• Students walking across campus
• Parents tracking their family's journeys
• Lone workers on the move

Street Shield is a safety awareness tool for informational purposes only. It does not replace or connect to professional services.

## Review Notes for App Store Reviewer

This app provides safety AWARENESS features for pedestrians, runners, and cyclists. It does NOT:
- Claim to be an emergency service
- Claim to be a medical device
- Call emergency numbers (911, 112, 999)
- Provide medical diagnoses or treatment recommendations
- Replace any professional service

The "Quick Alert" feature sends location-sharing notifications to user-specified trusted contacts (family/friends). It does not contact authorities or emergency services.

The "Activity Insights" feature displays informational movement data (rhythm, energy, alertness). It is NOT a medical device and does not provide medical advice. All data is for informational and entertainment purposes only.

The app includes a clear disclaimer visible to users: "This is a safety awareness tool for informational purposes only. It does not replace or connect to professional services."
