<product_requirements>
The Street Shield app aims to be a mobile safety solution for pedestrians and runners, providing critical spatial awareness and emergency support. It needs to auto-start, work on all platforms, and offer music-friendly voice alerts. Initial requirements included traffic/intersection analysis, emergency vehicle alerts, hazard detection (e.g., ice, weather), AI safety scoring, and smart alert prioritization.

Beyond the initial build, user requests significantly expanded the scope:
1.  **Emergency Contact Enhancement:** Voice-triggered functionality to notify contacts in casualty situations, with continuous monitoring and voice guidance. This evolved to require backend API integration, phone number validation, improved voice prompt clarity, and hands-free activation.
2.  **Electric Scooter Detection:** Identify silent, fast, and dangerous electric scooters, integrating with proximity threats and noise cancellation.
3.  **Accurate Health Monitoring:** Real sensor integration, medically-accurate thresholds, and advanced algorithms for heart rate, stress, blood oxygen, and activity.
4.  **Voice-Activated Information (Naked Info):** Hands-free audio information on demand, accessible at all times after activation, for users with headphones.
5.  **Cycling-Specific Features:** Comprehensive safety algorithms and protections tailored for cyclists.
6.  **Street Shield Trigger Word:** Establish the app's name as the primary voice trigger for information requests.
</product_requirements>
