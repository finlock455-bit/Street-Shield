*   **AI-Powered Safety Scoring:** Provide an overall safety assessment.
*   **Smart Alert Prioritization:** Deliver alerts without being overly bothersome.
*   **Emergency Contact Functionality:** Voice-triggered activation to notify emergency contacts with location in casualty situations (stuck by car, robbery, attack). This includes continuous monitoring and voice guidance during emergencies.

*   **Real-Time Accurate Weather Warnings:** Integrate live weather data (e.g., OpenWeatherMap) for precise hazard detection, with a robust simulation as a fallback.

*   **Biometric Integration for Health Monitoring:** Continuously monitor health metrics (heart rate, stress, blood oxygen, activity) and trigger emergency protocols for critical conditions.
